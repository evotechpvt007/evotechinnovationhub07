About EVOTECH

We build intelligent agri-tech solutions that make poultry farming more ethical and more profitable. Our focus: early, accurate egg fertility detection using computer vision.

Ethical First AI + Vision Real-time Scalable

Our system analyzes candling images taken on day 3 and classifies eggs as fertile or unfertile with confidence scores, enabling informed decisions before incubation continues.

  • Batch or single-egg scanning
  • Lightweight on-device inference
  • Simple dashboard and CSV export

Our Technology

How AI + candling work together in a fast, reliable pipeline.

🔦

High-contrast Candling

Uniform LED backlight reveals vascular patterns without heating the egg.

🤖

On-device AI

Optimized CNN model performs classification in ~1–2 seconds per egg.

📊

Actionable Output

Confidence score, batch summaries, and exportable reports for farm ops.
