AI Based Egg Fertility Detector

Smart IoT + AI solution for early egg fertility detection on day 3, designed for modern poultry farms.

Project Overview

AI-based egg fertility detection uses computer vision and deep learning to classify eggs as fertile or infertile from candling images.It delivers fast, accurate, and non-invasive results, reducing errors and labor costs.This improves hatchery efficiency, scalability, and overall hatch rates.

About the Project

Our project is an AI-based system** designed to detect egg fertility and predict chick gender on the third day after the egg is laid, allowing poultry farms to take early action. Using candling images , the system identifies embryo development and blood vessel patterns that indicate fertility. A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) processes these images to classify eggs as fertile or unfertile, and for fertile eggs, predicts whether the chick will be male or female. The hardware setup uses a Raspberry Pi with a camera, ultrasonic probe, and LED lighting to capture real-time data, which is then analyzed and displayed with a **confidence score**. The results are also logged in a CSV file for tracking and record-keeping. By enabling early separation of unfertile and male eggs, the project helps farms reduce feeding costs, improve hatchery efficiency, and avoid the ethical issues of culling male chicks after hatching.
