Purifying Belt Project

The Purifying Belt is a next-gen wearable designed to improve posture, reduce strain, and actively monitor user health metrics. With embedded sensors and AI algorithms, it detects posture issues in real-time and sends gentle haptic feedback to correct it.

Beyond posture correction, the belt integrates an air purification module, ensuring fresh and clean breathing air in urban environments.

Egg Fertility Prediction Project

Our AI-powered Egg Fertility Prediction System allows poultry farms to identify fertile eggs within 3 days of laying using advanced imaging and deep learning. This early detection prevents resource wastage and maximizes hatching efficiency.

The solution uses convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to analyze candling images, delivering high accuracy with real-time results.
